Why Traditional Methods Fail

Current disaster response suffers from fragmented information, delayed decisions, and inefficient resource allocation. Here's how our system solves these life-threatening gaps:

Problem: Siloed Agencies

NDRF, SDRF, hospitals, and NGOs often work without real-time coordination, leading to duplicated efforts and missed priorities.

Our Solution:

Unified command center with live data sharing between all agencies through secure APIs.

Problem: Delayed Response

Manual damage assessment and resource allocation can take 12-48 hours while victims suffer.

Our Solution:

AI analyzes satellite imagery and ground reports to predict needs within 30 minutes of disaster onset.

Problem: Wasted Resources

30-40% of emergency supplies are misallocated due to poor visibility into actual needs.

Our Solution:

Dynamic inventory management with real-time tracking of all resources from warehouse to victim.
